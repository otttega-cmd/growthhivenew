Post-event publication Market Global · Anchored in Lagos Format Three-day institutional forum

J.P. Morgan has been in Nigeria since the 1980s. For forty years, Lagos was a listening post — a representative office that sent capital elsewhere. In 2024, that changed. Jamie Dimon flew to Lagos. The CBN application for a full merchant banking licence was in motion. The bank was no longer watching Africa from a distance. It was about to be here.

The brief was simple in concept and ambitious in execution: mark this moment with something that matched its significance. Not a press release. Not a webinar. A physical event — invite-only, globally attended, intellectually serious — that positioned Lagos as the city from which JP Morgan Asset Management was choosing to look at the world's long-term investment picture.

Growthhive was engaged to conceive the event, build its identity, develop the invitation strategy, produce all physical and digital collateral, and design the post-event publication — JP Morgan Asset Management's first African flagship report.

Three days. One conviction.

03 — The Identity

An event identity
as serious as
the capital in the room.

Growthhive developed the full event identity for The Long View — the wordmark, the typographic system, the physical invitation, the programme, all venue signage, and the post-event publication. Every touchpoint was built to feel like it belonged at the intersection of JP Morgan's institutional authority and Lagos's specific, urgent energy.

The visual language is deliberately restrained. Deep navy as the ground. Old gold as the single accent. A high-contrast editorial serif for the event name and day titles. Space Mono for the analytical detail. Grid lines running beneath everything — the latent structure of a financial chart, made architectural.

The Invitation
Premium physical invitation, letterpress printed on 400gsm Invercote. Matte navy board, gold foil type, ribbon bound. Mailed to 280 select recipients across 18 countries.
Event Credential
Delegate badge and lanyard system. Individual credentials with attendee name, institution, and access tier (General · Fireside · Roundtable). 200 produced.
The Long View: Africa Capital Perspectives 2025
JP Morgan Asset Management's inaugural Africa report. Designed and produced by Growthhive. 48 pages, perfect bound. Seeded to all 200 attendees at close of forum.
